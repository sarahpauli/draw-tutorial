Cat

Follow this quick seven-step tutorial to learn how to draw a cat!

First, find a reference photo online of a cat. I found this one from Wikipedia!

Study your reference image until you have a good understanding of the animal you are about to draw.

Image by Hisashi from Japan, CC BY-SA 2.0, via Wikimedia Commons

Now that you have studied your image, begin drawing the head of your cat.

TIP: If you are having trouble starting, draw a circle as a base, then draw your ears and head shape. You can erase the circle after you are finished!

Once you are happy with your head shape, draw the body of the cat.

TIP: Try to draw a peanut shape for the body as a base!

Now that you have drawn the head and body, it's time to draw the front and back legs! Always check on your reference image if you need help.

Did you know? Cats spend about 70% of their life sleeping - what a life! Find more fun cat facts from Purina here.

Your cat's body is finished! Now, draw its face: include eyes, a nose, and a mouth. If you want to add detail, feel free to draw whiskers or fur marks.

Great, you have finished the outline of your cat! Now, feel free to add any color or personality to your drawing to make it uniquely yours.

Did you know? Cats share more than 95% of their genetics with tigers. Find more fun cat facts from Purina here.

You did it! Now you have a great piece of art that you can share with your friends and family. Feel free to return to steps in this tutorial, or go to another tutorial.
